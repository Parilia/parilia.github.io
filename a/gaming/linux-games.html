Great Linux Games

There are many great native Linux games which I feel get overlooked since proton became a staple in the Linux gaming scene. There are also some very big names that run natively on Linux such as Minecraft. Below you will find some of my favourite native Linux games. I feel I should also mention many games have Linux versions but if they don't work well or as well as they would otherwise on windows I will not be including them. I will also only be including games I have personally played.

Avorion

Avorion is a space based adventure game where you can craft your own ship and even your own fleet. To me it feels like a spiritual successor to freelancer. Building in the game gives you a lot of freedom and also thankfully has a steam workshop where you can painlessly grab ships and stations. My current play through I am going for a halo theme and I rather suck at the building so thankfully the workshop is there.

Multiplayer, PVE, PVP, CO-OP
https://www.avorion.net

Vintage Story

Vintage Story is wilderness survival crafting game, it feels like more of a challenge than your standard survival game. Crops grow slowly, trees grow slow and seasons are a big deal. You can definitely see that the game started its life as Minecraft mod but unlike Minecraft this game is not as forgiving. If you want a challenge and are a fan of open world crafting survival games then you will want to have a look at this one.

Multiplayer, PVE, PVP, CO-OP
https://www.vintagestory.at

Veloren

Veloren is an open world voxel based rpg written in rust. It is a fully open source game where assets such as music and graphics have been fully made by the community. It has been inspired by games such as Minecraft, breath of the wild and dwarf fortress. Personally I feel exploring this game world to be a fun and freeing experience and do highly recommend you give this game a try.

Multiplayer, PVE, CO-OP
https://veloren.net

Factorio

Factorio is a factory building game, to me it feels the OG factory game, mining resources, researching technologies, building infrastructure, automating production, and fighting enemies.

PVE, CO-OP
https://www.factorio.com

Stellaris

Stellaris is a space grand strategy game, it gives you so much freedom to make whatever race and empire you wish. From a peace loving corpo who's only goal is to make all the money to a species hell bent on the destruction of all sentient life, the choices are truly endless. "Discover a spectacular and ever-changing universe! Explore, discover and interact with a multitude of species as you journey among the stars. Forge a galactic empire as you delve into newly discovered planets, unearth treasures and establish a direction for your society. Uncover a grand strategy game that reaches the very edges of the universe."

Multiplayer, PVE, PVP, CO-OP
https://www.paradoxinteractive.com/games/stellaris
