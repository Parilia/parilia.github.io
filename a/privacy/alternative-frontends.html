Alternative Front-ends

A front-end is how you consume content, it is the user facing part of a website. For instance youtube.com is the official front-end for youtube. For a lot of popular websites there are alternative front-ends made by third parties, usually these provide a better user experience and don't tend to have ads or privacy invasive tracking. You should look at your browsers extensions as there may be one that allow auto redirects which take you to these front-ends.

Youtube

Invidious

Invidious is a privacy focused open source alternative front-end for youtube. It is decentralised so you will need to find a server to join if you want to have your subscriptions, you can also watch without logging in.

https://docs.invidious.io/instances
https://invidious.io

Freetube

Freetube unlike invidious is a program separate from your browser, I prefer this as it doesn't require you to log in. You can choose to use the youtube api or the invidious api. Freetube allows you to make multiple profiles which can have their own subscriptions and are all stored locally, another great feature is it remembers where you are in a playlist (something youtube still doesn't do).

https://freetubeapp.io
https://github.com/FreeTubeApp/FreeTube

Libretube (Android)

With LibreTube, you can freely watch and listen to content without the fear of prying eyes watching everything you are doing. Apart from that the Piped proxies can bypass geo restrictions, therefore LibreTube can be used in countries where YouTube is blocked.

https://libretube.dev
https://github.com/libre-tube/LibreTube

Yattee (iOS)

Yattee is a privacy oriented video player for iOS, tvOS and macOS.

https://github.com/yattee/yattee

Medium

Scribe

Scribe makes medium a less bad experience when people are forced to engage with it.

https://sr.ht/~edwardloveall/Scribehttps://scribe.rip

LibMedium

LibMedium is a decentralised privacy focused alternative front-end for medium.

https://github.com/realaravinth/libmedium

Google

I will start this by saying you really should be using another search engine such as Duckduckgo but sometimes you are forced onto google and for those times you should use...

LibreX

LibreX gives you results from Google, Qwant, Ahmia and popular torrent sites without spying on you and doesn't save any type of data about the user, there are no logs, no caches.

https://github.com/hnhx/librex

Google Translate

Lingva Translate

An alternative front-end for Google Translate, serving as a Free and Open Source translator with over a hundred languages available.

https://github.com/TheDavidDelta/lingva-translate

LibreTranslate

Free and Open Source Machine Translation API, entirely self-hosted.

https://github.com/LibreTranslate/LibreTranslate

Fandom

BreezeWiki

BreezeWiki removes ads, videos, and suggested content, leaving you with a clean page that doesn't slow down your device or use up your data.

https://breezewiki.com

Pintrest

Bintrest

Bintrest allows you to browse pintrest without ads and annoying popups

https://github.com/Ahwxorg/Binternet

Quora

Quetre

Quetre is an alternative front-end to Quora. It enables you to see answers without ads, trackers, and other such bloat.

https://github.com/zyachel/quetre
